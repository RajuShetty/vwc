Ministries

Children’s ministry

The Bible says in Psalm 127:4 that children are like arrows in a warrior’s hands. How true! At Vineyard Worker of Christ Church, the children are trained from their early age itself about the One True Living God – Jesus. Every Sunday morning, more than 1000 kids attend the Children’s church where they are taught the bible and the great Love of Jesus for us. According to their ages, the kids are categorized in groups namely Moses group, Daniel group, Benjamin group and Samuel group. We believe that as these kids grow up, they may one day stand as strong warriors for God’s Kingdom.

Women’s ministry

Led by Pastor Jayshree Silway, the Women’s ministry is a learning space for women from all walks of life on how to lead and live a Godly family life. Women who are also known as the “makers of homes” are introduced to their true identity in Jesus – Daughters of Christ!

Junior youth group

After getting graduated and equipped from the Children’s Church, the young warriors are not just left unattended to in the Vineyard Workers of Christ Church. An anointed Junior Youth group ministry takes the young souls into its bosom to nurture more efficiently into the Kingdom of Jesus. Led by the youth leader, the youngsters are given the teaching and guidance to steer their lives towards Godly success and Wisdom.
